FAQ

Common questions

How long does it take to set up?

Most schools are up and running within 15 minutes. We help you import your existing student data and configure classes, subjects, and fee structures.

Do I need to install anything?

No. SchoolManager runs entirely in the browser. Teachers, parents, and students can access it from any device — phone, tablet, or computer.

Is my school's data safe?

Absolutely. All data is encrypted, access is role-based, and no school can see another school's data. We take daily automated backups.

Can I export my data?

Yes. You can export student records, attendance, fee reports, and exam results at any time in standard formats. Your data is always yours.

What boards do you support?

CBSE, ICSE, and all state boards. The platform is flexible enough to handle any grading system, term structure, or report card format.

Is there a free plan?

Yes. Schools with up to 50 students can use SchoolManager for free, forever. No credit card required. See all plans.
