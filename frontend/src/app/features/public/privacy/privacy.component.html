Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that schools and their staff enter into the platform: student records, attendance data, fee records, exam results, and staff details. We also collect account information (name, email, phone) from users who register or request a demo.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve the SchoolManager service. We use it to display dashboards, generate reports, send notifications you configure, and provide customer support. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit (TLS 1.3). We use PostgreSQL with automated daily backups. Access to production data is restricted to essential personnel only.

4. Who Can See Your Data

Within your school, data visibility is controlled by role-based access. Teachers see only their assigned classes. Parents see only their own child's information. Students see only their own records. No other school on the platform can see your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account termination, data is retained for 60 days (to allow recovery), then permanently deleted. You may request immediate deletion by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your data at any time. School administrators can export all school data in standard formats. To exercise these rights, contact your school administrator or reach out to us directly.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies only — for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics that track individual users.

8. Children's Data

SchoolManager processes student data on behalf of schools. Schools are the data controllers for student information. We process this data only as directed by the school and in accordance with applicable education data privacy regulations.

9. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your information, we will notify affected schools within 72 hours of discovery, along with details of the breach and steps being taken.

10. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes to this policy via email or in-app notification at least 30 days before they take effect.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or data requests, contact us at privacy@schoolmanager.live.
