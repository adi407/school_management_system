About Us

Building the operating system
for modern Indian schools

SchoolManager was born from a simple observation: schools spend more time on paperwork than on students. We're here to change that.

Our Mission

To give every school — regardless of size or budget — access to the same caliber of management tools that top institutions use. We believe great education starts with well-run schools.

Based in India

SchoolManager is designed and built in India, for Indian schools. We understand the unique challenges of CBSE, ICSE, and state board schools — from attendance registers to fee collection cycles.

Technology

Built on enterprise-grade technology — Spring Boot, Angular, and PostgreSQL — with the same architectural patterns used by companies like Notion and Linear. Your data is secure, your school runs fast.

Who We Serve

From 50-student primary schools to 2,000+ student senior secondary institutions. Whether you're a single school or a chain of schools, SchoolManager scales to fit your needs.

What We Believe

Your data, your control

Schools own their data completely. Export everything, anytime. No lock-in.

Simple over clever

Every feature should be usable by a teacher who has never used software before.

Privacy by default

Student data is sensitive. We treat it with the same care we'd want for our own children.
